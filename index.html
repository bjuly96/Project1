Brain Computer Interface

The Center for Sensorimotor Neural Engineering is working to implement an

implantable brain chip that can record neural electrical signals and transmit

them to receivers in the limb, bypassing the damage and restoring movement.

The technology, known as a brain-computer interface, records and transmits

signals through electrodes, which are tiny pieces of material that read

signals from brain chemicals known as neurotransmitters. The current

material for electrodes in these devices is thin-film platinum. One of the

problems with this device is that electrodes can fracture and fall over time.

Researchers are using this technology to record neural signals both along the

the brain's cortical surface and from inside the brain at the same time.

Artificial Intelligence

Self-learning systems, or Artificial Intelligence, are changing the way architecture is practiced. Artificial Intelligence is growing rapidly and this is

because of the constant innovation in the speed microprocessors, which will increase the volume of data that can be gathered and stored.

The architectural design process can be influenced by AI at different construction stages as follows: Site and social search, Design decision-

making, Client and user engagement, Realizing designs and rise of robot craftsmen, and Integrating AI Systems. Software such as EcoDesigner

Star open-source plugins for Google SketchUp allows architects to immediately calculate necessary building and environmental analyses without

ever having to leave their office. Software such as Space Syntax's 'depthmathX' affects Artificial Intelligence within Design decision-making by

analyzing the spatial network of a city with an aim to understand and utilize social interactions and in the design process. Through AI, augmented

reality helps client and user engagement enable people to engage with a design prior to construction. Within realizing designs and rise of robot

craftsmen, 3D printing technology developed from AI software catalyzes a resurgence of detail and ornamentation, now rare due to the decline in

traditional crafts. Integrating AI systems to develop simple AI systems to run his home and help with his day-to-day work. It would have the

ability to recognize the voices of members of the household and respond to their requests.
